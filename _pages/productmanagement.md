### Wouldn't it be easier/better/cheaper if we could...

So many great ideas start like this, then sink without trace. Why? Because it turns out that there's usually some hidden complexity. Or everyone sees something happening and jumps on the bandwagon - before you know it, you're in design-by-committee and Fred in finance\* is lobbying for it to improve his book-keeping when the original idea was to streamline bookings for beneficiary events.

*Product Management* is the art of taking all that noise, cutting through it to find what people actually need, then getting that built fast. Once it's built, you've got something to look at and iterate on until it actually _meets the needs_ of your organisation.
