- **Process development workshops**: We'll get your team in a room and work through the questions you need the data to answer. Then we'll co-create the processes that you need to ensure that it does.
- **Systems integration**: Got an existing system that you want to connect to a new one? We'll look at the cost-benefit and, if it makes sense, we'll spec out the best-value approach to getting data moving in the right way.
- **System roadmap coaching**: We'll sit down with your tech leadership and help map out what your tech needs to deliver for you over the medium to long term. Then we'll provide impartial advice as you go through procurement. Ultimately, you need to own your own procurement decisions, but we find it can help to have an independent sounding-board as you go through the process.
- **Data strategy development**: We'll work with your tech/data leadership to identify the questions your organization needs to answer. Then we'll look at your existing tech stack, determine where the gaps are and help you build a strategy to take to your trustees.
- **Bespoke consultancy**: Got a feeling that you need some external help, but you're not sure what it looks like? Let's have a chat and work through the options together!
